Poop-A-Day - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

Poop-A-Day ("the App") is developed by Sozo Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Data Stored on Your Device

All personal data you create within the App (bathroom visit logs, statistics, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers. If you delete the App, this data is permanently removed.

Data Export and Import

The App allows you to export your data as a JSON file and import it on another device. This process is entirely controlled by you. We do not receive, store, or process any exported data.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (based on IP)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, the App requests your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites using Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. This tracking is used solely for delivering personalized advertisements through Google AdMob. You can choose to allow or deny this request, and you can change your preference at any time in your device settings.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your bathroom visit logs or statistics
  • Your location
  • Any health or medical data

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements. AdMob Privacy Info

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
